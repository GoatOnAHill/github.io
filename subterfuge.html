Subterfuge



Main Story



One of the stories within the "Stratagem" saga. As the Pale Insurrection rages on, events behind the scenes align. Power is exchanged, pieces move into place and the stage is set for one of the greatest battles in Coalition memory.

Prologue: Sethran is a courier for the ATTC; he is running cargo along the light-rails and is ambushed by raiders who were tipped off about his run.

Arc 1: With the help of the on-board AI "Maybe Road Life's Not So Bad" (Mabel), Sethran makes it to Sigma Relay Station 003. He finds that the station is overrun by insurrectionists.

Arc 2: Aiding a hidden resistance cell on the space station, Sethran is embroiled in a deadly political plot. Meanwhile, Mabel escapes deletion and stumbles across a horrifying secret.

Arc 3: Leaving the Relay Staion behind him, Sethran continues his long journey to complete his cargo run. This time, however, he has a few new additions to his crew.




Side-Stories



Stratagem also has a number of spin-off tales that fill in some of the blanks left by the main narrative.

The Fallen: Aiden, a Priddin archaeologist, makes an incredible discovery in an ancient Precursor ruin. As his life spirals into turmoil, a hidden hand begins to tease at the strings of his fate.

Memories Part 1: Mabel is caught up over a data fragment that she can't seem to ignore. What are these names? These places? What do these recordings hide?

Memories Part 2: With Mabel digging through the fragmented data in her memory banks, Maia gets lost in her own puzzle. A name is stuck in her head, but What is its significance?

The Lost: A nameless being wakes up in soundless void. He wanders Otherworld without a face, drawn by invisible string to places unknown. What will become of this being without a face? What is drawing him onward in his seemingly fruitless journey?
